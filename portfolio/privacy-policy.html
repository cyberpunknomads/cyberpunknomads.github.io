Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Portfolio Keeper mobile application ("Service"). The Service is provided solely for personal entertainment and informal record-keeping and has no financial, investment, or advisory function whatsoever. The data controller is the individual developer of Portfolio Keeper, reachable via the Privacy Contact form in the app Settings. No data protection officer has been appointed. This policy is governed by Swedish law.

Age

The Service is not intended for persons under the age of 18. The developer makes no representation that the Service is appropriate for any particular age group. It is the responsibility of the user to determine suitability.

What Is Collected

The following personal data is processed:

• Email address — provided at account registration.
• Portfolio data — stock holdings and transactions, manually entered by the user.
• Journal entries — trading notes and reflections, manually entered by the user.
• Goals — financial goals, manually entered by the user.
• Authentication tokens — generated upon login, stored in device secure local storage.
• Billing and subscription status — processed by the applicable app store platform (Apple App Store or Google Play). The developer does not receive or store full payment card details.
• Crash diagnostic data — when an unhandled error occurs, the error message, stack trace, device type, OS version, and app version are transmitted to Sentry for debugging purposes. No portfolio data, financial figures, or user-identifiable information is included in these reports.

No analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, or device tracking data are collected. The developer does not currently engage in any form of user tracking.

Purposes and Legal Bases

Data is processed for the following purposes, each based on performance of the contract between the user and the developer (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)):

• Email address — to create and maintain the user account.
• Portfolio data, journal entries, and goals — to provide the core storage and display function of the Service.
• Authentication tokens — to authenticate the user session.
• Billing and subscription status — to verify access to premium features. This data is processed by the applicable app store platform; the developer receives only confirmation of subscription status.

Provision of an email address is a contractual requirement for use of the Service. Users who do not provide it cannot use the Service.

Data Storage

Data is stored on infrastructure provided by Supabase, operated within the European Economic Area. Security measures applicable to that infrastructure are Supabase's responsibility under their own terms and documentation. The developer makes no representation, warranty, or guarantee regarding the security, availability, or integrity of data stored on third-party infrastructure.

Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as the user maintains an account. Upon account deletion request submitted via the Privacy Contact form in app Settings, account data will be removed from active systems. Removal from backup systems may take additional time. The developer retains the right to retain data to the extent required by applicable law or for the establishment, exercise, or defence of legal claims.

Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party services:

• Supabase — data storage and authentication infrastructure. See supabase.com/privacy.
• Apple App Store / Google Play — subscription billing and payment processing for premium features. The developer does not receive full payment card details. See apple.com/legal/privacy and policies.google.com/privacy.
• Sentry — crash and error reporting. When the application encounters an unhandled error, diagnostic information is transmitted to Sentry, Inc. This includes the error message, stack trace, device type, operating system version, and app version. No portfolio data, financial figures, journal entries, or user-identifiable information is transmitted. IP addresses are not collected. See sentry.io/privacy.

The Service retrieves publicly available market data from third-party sources. No personal data is transmitted to those sources. The developer is not responsible for the data practices of any third-party service.

No Tracking

The Service does not use analytics, advertising networks, or third-party tracking SDKs of any kind. No tracking of users across apps or websites occurs. The crash reporting service (Sentry) described above is used solely for diagnosing application errors and does not track user behaviour or activity.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights under GDPR with respect to your personal data:

• Right of access (Art. 15)
• Right to rectification (Art. 16)
• Right to erasure (Art. 17)
• Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18)
• Right to data portability (Art. 20)

To exercise any of these rights, use the Privacy Contact form in app Settings (Settings → Privacy Contact). You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten, imy.se).
