Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21 May 2026  |  Last Updated: 21 May 2026

Controller: Portfolio Keeper (individual developer)  |  Contact: privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com

Portfolio Keeper is built on a straightforward principle: we collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the app, and nothing more. We do not sell your data, display advertising, or share your information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. We do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not use any advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking technologies. This policy explains what we collect, why, how it is protected, and what rights you have. By using Portfolio Keeper you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Nothing in this policy limits or excludes any rights you have under applicable mandatory law, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This Privacy Policy is governed by Swedish law. The developer has not appointed a Data Protection Officer; all privacy-related queries should be directed to privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com.

Age Requirement

Portfolio Keeper is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. By creating an account or otherwise using the app you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we become aware, or have reason to believe, that a user is under 18, we will suspend the account and delete the associated personal data as promptly as reasonably practicable, and in any event within 30 days of becoming aware. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com.

What We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service. Specifically:

• Email address — collected when you register an account. Used solely for authentication, password reset, and optional service-related notifications you have consented to receive.

• Portfolio data you enter manually — this includes stock ticker symbols, share quantities, purchase prices, purchase dates, and any notes or labels you choose to enter. This data is created entirely by you and is used only to display it back to you within the app.

• Authentication tokens — short-lived session tokens stored in your device's secure local storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) by the Supabase authentication library, used to keep you logged in between sessions.

We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, location, device identifiers, advertising IDs (IDFA, GAID), biometric data, contacts, photos, browsing history, or any financial account credentials. We do not use advertising networks, third-party analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting services that collect personal data.

How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes and on the following legal bases under GDPR:

• Authentication and account management (email) — necessary for the performance of our contract with you (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).
• Password reset and security communications (email) — necessary for the performance of our contract with you (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).
• Optional summary or notification emails (email) — only if you have explicitly opted in; based on your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)). You may withdraw consent at any time via Settings or by contacting privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com.
• Displaying your portfolio data to you — necessary for the performance of our contract with you (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)).
• Closing inactive accounts — based on our legitimate interests in storage minimisation under GDPR Art. 5(1)(e).

We do not use your data for profiling, advertising, or any purpose not listed above. We do not make automated decisions about you that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers operated by Supabase, Inc., located within the European Union, under a GDPR-compliant Data Processing Agreement. All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. All data stored on Supabase infrastructure is encrypted at rest. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) so that your data is accessible only to your authenticated account.

We take reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, we are not liable for security breaches that are beyond our reasonable control, provided that we have implemented the measures described in this policy.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your personal data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time via Settings. Upon deletion, your email address and portfolio data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Authentication tokens stored locally on your device are cleared immediately upon sign-out or deletion.

To request deletion: use the Delete Account function in Settings, or email privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" from your registered email address. We will confirm deletion within 30 days.

Accounts inactive for 36 or more consecutive months are considered dormant. We will email you at least 30 days before closing a dormant account. This processing is based on our legitimate interests in storage minimisation (GDPR Art. 5(1)(e)).

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

• Supabase, Inc. — cloud database and authentication. Acts as our data processor under a GDPR-compliant DPA. EU infrastructure. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

• Yahoo Finance public API — used to retrieve publicly available stock price data. Only stock ticker symbols are transmitted; no personal data is ever sent to Yahoo Finance. Yahoo Finance data may be delayed, inaccurate, incomplete, or unavailable. Yahoo Finance is an independent third-party data source, not a data processor under this policy.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or crash-reporting services that collect or transmit personal data.

No Tracking

Portfolio Keeper does not track you across third-party apps or websites. We do not use advertising identifiers, cross-site tracking cookies, fingerprinting, or any similar technology to identify or follow you outside of the app. This disclosure is made in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework and Google Play's data safety requirements.

• Right of access — request a copy of your personal data.

Nothing in this Privacy Policy overrides, limits, or waives any rights you have under GDPR or other applicable mandatory law.
