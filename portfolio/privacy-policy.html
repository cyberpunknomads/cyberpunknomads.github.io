Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Portfolio Keeper is built on a straightforward principle: we collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the app, and nothing more. We do not sell your data, display advertising, or share your information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what rights you have. By using Portfolio Keeper you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Nothing in this policy limits or excludes any rights you have under applicable mandatory law, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Age Requirement

Portfolio Keeper is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. By creating an account or otherwise using the app you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we become aware, or have reason to believe, that a user is under 18, we will suspend the account and delete the associated data as promptly as reasonably practicable. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com.

What We Collect

We collect and process only the minimum personal data required to provide the service:

• Email address — used solely for account authentication and, if you choose to enable them, optional summary notifications.
• Portfolio data (ticker symbols, quantities, and purchase prices that you enter manually) — stored solely to display that data back to you within the app.

We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, precise or approximate location, device identifiers, advertising IDs, biometric data, browsing history, or any other category of personal data beyond those listed above. All data you enter is data you have chosen to provide. We make no attempt to infer, derive, or enrich your profile.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes and no others:

• Authentication and account security — your email address enables you to log in and to receive password-reset messages.
• Service delivery — your portfolio data is stored and retrieved solely so the app can display it back to you. We do not analyse, aggregate, or derive insights from your portfolio for any purpose other than displaying it to you.
• Optional communications — if you opt in, we may send periodic summary emails to your registered address. You may withdraw this consent at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

We do not use your data for profiling, advertising, sale to third parties, or any purpose not listed above. The legal bases for processing under GDPR are: performance of the contract between you and us (authentication, service delivery) and, where applicable, your consent (optional communications).

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on infrastructure provided by Supabase, Inc., operating within the European Union. We use row-level security (RLS) policies so that each user can access only their own data. All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We review and update our security practices on a best-effort basis.

While we take reasonable and industry-standard steps to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet and no method of electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and we expressly disclaim liability for unauthorised access to, alteration of, disclosure of, or destruction of your data that results from circumstances beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to third-party infrastructure failures, zero-day vulnerabilities, or force majeure events. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal data only for as long as your account remains active or as otherwise required by law. You may permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time through the Settings screen within the app. Following a deletion request, we will remove your data from our active systems within 30 days, subject to any shorter or longer period required by applicable law.

Accounts that have shown no sign-in activity for a continuous period of three (3) years or more are considered inactive. We will send a notice to your registered email address at least 30 days before closing an inactive account. If no response or activity is recorded within that 30-day period, the account and all associated data will be deleted.

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of third-party services that are necessary to operate the app:

• Yahoo Finance (public API) — the app retrieves publicly available market price data using only ticker symbols. No personal data, account information, or any data that identifies you is transmitted to Yahoo Finance. Price data obtained from Yahoo Finance may be delayed, incomplete, or inaccurate. We have no affiliation with Yahoo Finance and are not responsible for the availability, accuracy, or continuity of their data.

• Supabase, Inc. — acts as our data processor under a data processing agreement consistent with GDPR requirements, storing your email address and portfolio data on EU infrastructure. Their privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, crash-reporting services that collect personal data, or any other third-party service that processes your personal data, beyond those listed above.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

• Right of access — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
• Right to rectification — you may ask us to correct inaccurate data. (Most of your data can be corrected directly within the app.)
• Right to erasure — you may request deletion of your personal data. You can exercise this right directly via the Settings screen; you may also contact us.
• Right to data portability — you may request your data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
• Right to restrict processing — you may ask us to limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
• Right to object — you may object to processing carried out on the basis of our legitimate interests.
• Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days. If you believe we have not handled your data in accordance with applicable law, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Authority for Privacy Protection (Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten, imy.se), or with the supervisory authority in your country of residence.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make a material change — meaning a change that meaningfully alters your rights or our obligations — we will notify you within the app before the change takes effect. Continued use of the app after the effective date of a material change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
