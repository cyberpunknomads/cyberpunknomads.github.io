Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 21 May 2026  |  Last Updated: 21 May 2026

Controller: Portfolio Keeper (individual developer)  |  Contact: privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com

Portfolio Keeper is built on a straightforward principle: we collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the app, and nothing more. We do not sell your data, display advertising, or share your information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. We do not track you across other apps or websites, and we do not use any advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking technologies. This policy explains what we collect, why, how it is protected, and what rights you have. By using Portfolio Keeper you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Nothing in this policy limits or excludes any rights you have under applicable mandatory law, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). This Privacy Policy is governed by Swedish law. The developer has not appointed a Data Protection Officer; all privacy-related queries should be directed to privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com.

Age Requirement

Portfolio Keeper is intended exclusively for users who are 18 years of age or older. By creating an account or otherwise using the app you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will suspend the account and delete the associated personal data as promptly as reasonably practicable. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com.

What We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

• Email address — collected when you register. Used solely for authentication, password reset, and optional notifications you have consented to receive.

• Portfolio data you enter manually — stock ticker symbols, share quantities, purchase prices, purchase dates, and any notes you choose to enter. Used only to display it back to you within the app.

• Authentication tokens — short-lived session tokens stored in your device's secure local storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) by the Supabase authentication library, used to keep you logged in between sessions.

We do not collect your name, phone number, physical address, location, device identifiers, advertising IDs (IDFA, GAID), biometric data, contacts, photos, browsing history, or any financial account credentials. We do not use advertising networks, third-party analytics SDKs, or crash-reporting services that collect personal data.

How We Use Your Data

We use data only for the following purposes, on the following GDPR legal bases:

• Authentication and account management (email) — performance of contract, GDPR Art. 6(1)(b).
• Password reset and security communications (email) — performance of contract, GDPR Art. 6(1)(b).
• Optional summary or notification emails (email) — consent, GDPR Art. 6(1)(a). You may withdraw consent at any time via Settings or by contacting privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com.
• Displaying your portfolio data to you — performance of contract, GDPR Art. 6(1)(b).
• Closing inactive accounts — legitimate interests in storage minimisation, GDPR Art. 5(1)(e).

We do not use your data for profiling, advertising, or any purpose not listed above. We process your data only as described in this policy and in accordance with applicable data protection law. We do not make automated decisions about you that produce legal or similarly significant effects.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers operated by Supabase, Inc., located within the European Union, under a GDPR-compliant Data Processing Agreement. All data in transit is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. All data stored on Supabase infrastructure is encrypted at rest. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) so that your data is accessible only to your authenticated account.

We implement security measures that we consider appropriate for the nature, scope, and purposes of our processing. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data. We are not liable for security incidents caused by events beyond our reasonable control. This policy does not constitute a warranty as to the adequacy of our security measures.

Data Retention & Deletion

Your personal data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time via Settings. Upon deletion, we will process your request and remove your personal data in accordance with our obligations under Article 17 GDPR. Residual copies may remain in encrypted backup systems for a limited period as part of our standard automated backup rotation before being permanently overwritten. We reserve the right to retain data where required to do so by law or for legitimate legal defence purposes.

To request deletion: use the Delete Account function in Settings, or contact privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com. We will endeavour to respond in accordance with our obligations under applicable data protection law.

We reserve the right to close accounts that have been inactive for an extended period. We will endeavour to provide advance notice where reasonably practicable.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

• Supabase, Inc. — cloud database and authentication. Acts as our data processor under a GDPR-compliant DPA. EU infrastructure. Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

• Yahoo Finance public API — used to retrieve publicly available stock price data. Only stock ticker symbols are transmitted; no personal data is ever sent to Yahoo Finance. Yahoo Finance data may be delayed, inaccurate, incomplete, or unavailable. Yahoo Finance is an independent third-party data source, not a data processor under this policy.

We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising networks, social media SDKs, or crash-reporting services that collect or transmit personal data.

No Tracking

Portfolio Keeper does not track you across third-party apps or websites. We do not use advertising identifiers, cross-site tracking cookies, fingerprinting, or any similar technology to identify or follow you outside of the app. This disclosure is made in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework and Google Play's data safety requirements.
