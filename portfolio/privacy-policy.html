Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Portfolio Keeper is designed with a simple principle: we collect only what is strictly necessary to make the app work, and nothing more. We do not sell your data, show you ads, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Age Requirement

This app is intended for users aged 18 and over. By using Portfolio Keeper you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will delete their account and associated data promptly.

What We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to operate the service:

• Email address — used only for account authentication and optional portfolio summary emails you explicitly opt into.
• Portfolio data — stock symbols, quantities, prices, and journal entries that you enter yourself. This data is stored solely to provide the app's functionality to you.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, device identifiers, browsing history, or any other personal information beyond the above.

How We Use Your Data

Your email is used to:

• Authenticate your account.
• Send password-reset emails when you request them.
• Send weekly or monthly portfolio summary emails, but only if you explicitly enable this in Settings.

Your portfolio data is used exclusively to display your portfolio back to you. We do not analyse it, benchmark it against other users, or use it for any purpose other than powering your personal view of the app.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Supabase, a cloud database provider, using Row-Level Security policies that ensure no user can access another user's data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. We apply industry-standard security practices and review them regularly.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Delete My Account. We may close inactive accounts (no login for 3+ years) after giving 30 days' notice by email.

After account deletion, all your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. We do not retain backups of individual user data after deletion.

Third-Party Services

Portfolio Keeper fetches real-time and historical price data from Yahoo Finance's public API. We do not send any of your personal data to Yahoo Finance — only the ticker symbols you look up are included in those requests, and those are made directly from your device.

Authentication and database services are provided by Supabase (supabase.com). Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf under a signed Data Processing Agreement. Their infrastructure is located in the EU. Their privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have the following rights:

• Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
• Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data (you can do this directly in the app).
• Right to erasure — delete your account and all data via Settings, or by contacting us.
• Right to data portability — export your portfolio and journal data in machine-readable format via Settings → Export My Data.
• Right to restrict processing — contact us to request restricted processing.
• Right to object — contact us to object to processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy.portfoliokeeper@gmail.com

We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Swedish Data Protection Authority (Integritetsskyddsmyndigheten, imy.se).

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you via the app before the changes take effect. Continued use of the app after that date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
